Pizza Recipe

Description

Pizza is a dish of Italian origin consisting of a usually round, flat base of leavened wheat-based dough topped with tomatoes, cheese, and often various other ingredients, which is then baked at a high temperature, traditionally in a wood-fired oven. A small pizza is sometimes called a pizzetta

What is special about pizza? Humans are drawn to foods that are fatty, sweet, rich and complex. Pizza has all of these components. Cheese is fatty, meat toppings tend to be rich and the sauce is sweet. Pizza toppings are also packed with a compound called glutamate, which can be found in the tomatoes, cheese, pepperoni and sausage

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups (355ml) warm water (105F - 115F)
  • 1 package dry yeast
  • 3 3/4 (490g) bread flour
  • 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil (omit if cooking pizza in a wood-fired pizza oven)
  • 2 tablespoons kosher salt
  • 1 teaspoon sugar

Steps

  1. Proof the yeast
  2. Make and knead the pizza dough
  3. Make the dough rise
  4. Preheat thte oven
  5. Divide the dough into two balls
  6. Prep the toppins
  7. Flatten the ball and stretch out into a round
  8. Brush the dough with olive oil
  9. Sprinkle the pizza peel with cornmeal, put flattened dough on top
  10. Spread with tomato sauce and sprinkle toppings
  11. Slide Pizza into oven
  12. Bake
